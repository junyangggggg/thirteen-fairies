Introduction

"Thirteen Fairies" is a group of 13 people from 6A Xiao'20, CHKL. Some of these fairies are shy, most of them are noisy and naughty, but still, maintaining good academic performance. Every fairy in this group is talented. Some of them are excellent in music, literature, language and other fields. What miraculous about this group is, unlike other groups in 6A Xiao, most of them are not familiar with each other before 6A Xiao. However, the Covid-19 pandemic has brought them together. Now, they have graduated from high school and stepped into the university. Although some of them will study overseas, the bond never breaks (hopefully).
